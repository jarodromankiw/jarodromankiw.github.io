Vodka Pasta

Serves 2-3 people

Ingredients

  • 1/2 white onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (EVOO)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • 3 oz heavy cream
  • 1 oz vodka
  • Reserved pasta water (as needed)
  • 8 oz dry pasta

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta according to package instructions. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain and set aside.
  2. Step 2: In a large skillet over medium heat, add EVOO and diced onion. Cook for 3â€“4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until soft and translucent.
  3. Step 3: Add minced garlic and cook for about 30 seconds, until fragrant. Stir in red pepper flakes.
  4. Step 4: Add the tomato paste and cook for 3 minutes, stirring often, until it darkens slightly and caramelizes.
  5. Step 5: Stir in the heavy cream and vodka, mixing well to combine into a smooth sauce.
  6. Step 6: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time, stirring, until the sauce reaches your desired consistency.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot, optionally garnished with Parmesan cheese or fresh basil.
